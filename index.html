2024 Annual Numbers!

At Sacred Connections Community Church, we are proud of the impact we’ve made in our community this year. With the help of our dedicated volunteers and supporters, we distributed 296,000 pounds of food to ensure that no one in our community goes hungry. Through these efforts, we served 22,615 adults and 1,467 children across 2,080 unique households a total of 15,510 times.

Our mission to create access to nutritious food is strengthened by the 1,845 volunteer hours contributed by individuals passionate about building a stronger, healthier community. In addition to our food pantry services, we provided a warm, welcoming space where neighbors could share a hot meal and coffee alongside Food for Lane County distributions.

Strengthening Our Food Service through Partnership

For years, our Rack of Possibility has been a beacon of generosity, providing food and essentials to those in need—stocked both by our church and our community. Thanks to the dedication of two incredible volunteers and our partnership with Food for Lane County (FFLC), we’ve now been able to enclose the rack, protecting the food from rain and sun while continuing to serve our neighbors with dignity and care.

This upgrade ensures that our shared commitment to kindness and nourishment remains strong, no matter the weather. A heartfelt thank you to all who contribute—your generosity makes a difference every day!

Welcome to Our Event Space

We are grateful to offer our community a versatile event space, perfect for gatherings of all kinds. Whether you’re hosting a wedding, a celebration, a performance, or a special event, we offer a flexible and inviting environment to suit your needs. With a total of 1,781 sq. ft. of space, including a 525 sq. ft. stage, we provide a welcoming environment for your special occasion.

Features of Our Space:

Stage for Celebration: A 525 sq. ft. stage, ideal for performances, ceremonies, or worship, allowing you to create a meaningful experience for your gathering.

Adjustable Lighting to Set the Mood: Create a serene atmosphere with our adjustable lighting to set the tone for reflection, joy, or celebration.

Hospitality at Heart: Enjoy our complimentary water station, as well as 2-stall unisex restroom. A seperate ADA compliant bathroom is also available. 

Space for All Occasions: Whether you need the full 1,781 sq. ft. with the stage or the 1,256 sq. ft. main floor, our space can be arranged to suit the needs of your gathering, large or small.

We hope that our location brings people together in unity, love, and shared purpose for blessings and memorable moments. Contact us today to learn more about how we can support your event!
